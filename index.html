Medway's Most Trusted Minibus Operator

Founded in 2015 in the heart of Medway, Kent, London Minibus Transport has spent over a decade building a reputation for one thing: showing up on time, every time.

We specialise exclusively in 16-seater minibus hire — the sweet spot for most groups. Big enough for the whole team, easy to park, comfortable for every journey.
