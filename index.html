About Us

Medway's Most Trusted Minibus Operator

Based in the heart of Medway, Kent, Medway Minibus Transport has been getting groups where they need to go for over 20 years. Two decades of punctuality, professionalism and word-of-mouth reputation.

We specialise exclusively in 16-seater minibus hire — the sweet spot for most groups. Big enough for the whole team, easy to park, comfortable for every journey whether local or long-distance.

From school runs across Medway to airport transfers, London day trips and beyond — our experienced driver knows every road in Kent and the South East inside out.
