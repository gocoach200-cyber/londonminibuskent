About Us

Medway's Trusted Minibus Specialists

Based in the heart of Medway, Kent, London Minibus Transport has been getting groups where they need to go for over a decade. What started as a passion for providing genuinely reliable transport has grown into one of the most trusted minibus operators in the region.

We specialise in 16-seater minibus hire — the perfect size for most groups. Big enough for a team, small enough to park anywhere. We know the roads between Kent, London and the rest of the UK inside out, and we pride ourselves on punctuality, professionalism and a smooth journey every time.

Whether it's a local trip across Kent or a long-distance run to Scotland, our experienced driver will get you there safely, comfortably and on time.
